# PROJECT BRIEF｜GOODMATES Grab & Go

## One-line definition

GOODMATES Grab & Go is a London City Asian grab-and-go concept store offering chilled ready meals, fast reheating, self-ordering, app pre-ordering, office orders, coffee, milk tea and one in-store smart vending machine.

## First store

- Location: London City / financial district
- Size: 80–100 sqm
- Front-of-house: approx. 70%
- Back-of-house: approx. 30%
- Opening hours: Monday–Friday 10:00–20:00; Saturday 11:00–16:00 test trading
- Core target: office workers, finance/legal/professional services, corporate teams
- Core objective: user feedback + validated commercial model

## Offer

- Main meals: £6.6 / £7.7 / £8.8 / £9.9, VAT included
- Coffee: £2.99–£3.50
- Milk tea: £3.90 / £4.50 / £4.90
- Cuisine direction: Chinese + Japanese/Korean + Southeast Asian + South Asian
- First SKU range: 10–12 main-meal SKUs
- Halal: only selected certified meals, clearly labelled

## Store model

- No alcohol
- No open flame
- No chef-dependent kitchen
- Third-party central kitchen supply
- Chilled ready meals
- Microwave reheating
- Cashless payment
- Touchscreen ordering
- App pre-ordering
- Office orders
- In-store smart vending machine for peak-hour overflow
