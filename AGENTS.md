# Codex / Agent Instructions

## Project

GOODMATES Grab & Go — London City first-store rollout.

## Language

Primary working language: Chinese. Business-facing English may be used for supplier emails, contracts, checklists and UK compliance documents.

## Project rules

- Do not describe the full commercial plan to suppliers unless explicitly instructed.
- Central kitchen communication should be framed as a phased ready-meal supply project.
- Treat food safety, allergens, shelf life, traceability and cold chain as high-priority.
- Do not claim the whole store is halal. Only selected certified meals may be labelled as halal-certified.
- Avoid saying this is a hot food takeaway shop. Preferred wording: grab-and-go café / Asian lunch store / eat-in with ancillary takeaway.
- Financial assumptions should be conservative and clearly marked as assumptions.
