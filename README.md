# GOODMATES Grab & Go

GOODMATES Grab & Go is the execution workspace for the London City / financial district first-store rollout.

## Project purpose

Build and launch a lightweight Asian grab-and-go food retail concept in London, using a third-party central kitchen, chilled ready meals, fast reheating, self-ordering, app pre-ordering, office orders, machine-made drinks and one in-store smart vending machine.

## Core positioning

**A warm, modern, high-efficiency Asian grab-and-go brand for London’s everyday office lunch.**

Consumer promise:

**Under £10, fast, clean, warm, good-value Asian meals for City workers.**

## Working principle

Do not overbuild the first store. The first GOODMATES Grab & Go store exists to validate product-market fit, SKU performance, peak-hour service flow, central kitchen supply reliability, unit economics, office lunch repeat purchase, and future multi-site replication.
